Overview

Purpose

A central source to reveal popular and creative travel destinations found in all regions of the United States. Destination Hub 1.0 will first feature the most popular destinations for each western state. As time goes on, destinations will be added for the midwest, south, and all eastern states!

Audience

Everyone who has a passion and interest in travel is welcome to browse Destination Hub. We are committed to helping anyone who seeks a dynamic way to experience the United States!

Dynamic elements

Destination Hub will prioritize accessibility by using effective UX design elements such as large, clear images and matching colors to help information stand out at ease. The following will be implemented:

#1: Image Slider - An image slider will give the user control to quickly browse through destinations and obtain all information about them within a single arrow click. It will match the aesthetically-pleasing theme of the Destination Hub site.

#2: Drop-down menu - An interative menu on the home page will provide options to browse destinations from various states. Names of available states will be displayed on click..

#3: Smooth transitions - A simple yet impactful feature to make the site more user-friendly.

About Us/Contact

What are we about?

We are a website that aims to provide a central source to reveal popular and creative travel destinations found in all regions of the United States. We understand that traveling is a huge part of our daily lives. We are committed to helping anyone who seeks a dynamic way to experience the United States!

How was Destination Hub founded?

Founded in 2024, Destination Hub started as an idea for Kyle Amerjan, a man who was passionate about traveling around the United States. As motivation to travel grew, Kyle began researching destinations in various states. He created a plan for himself, but then thought, 'What if we could help others achieve the same?' He already organized various travel ideas and enjoyed saving pictures of what he would be traveling to. For this reason, he decided to create Destination Hub. Today, it continues to grow as a means of becoming one of the greatest organized sources to discover destinations all around the United States.
